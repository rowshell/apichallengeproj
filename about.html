About

Black Market Buddy is a presentation and evaluation of the impact that items introduced in the game mode Black Market Brawlers had upon different champions.


Each item has its own page, complete with an organizable table of champions and statistics. We have provided analysis of Win Rate and KDA both before the introduction of the game mode and during it. In order to maintain accurate results, only summoners which purchased a single Black Market Item were included in the statistics. Our goal is to provide an easy way to tell whether or not an item was beneficial or detrimental to each champion.


From the item page, a champion page can be accessed. Along with displaying a purty picture, the item which proved to help the champion the most (in terms of Win Rate and KDA) and the item which hurt the champion the most are displayed. This is determined through a comparison of Win Rate and KDA both before and during the game mode.


To find out more on the technical details behind the Black Market Buddy project, click on the How It Works button in the navigation bar above.
